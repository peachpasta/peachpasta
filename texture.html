Distortion :P

For this piece, I took my first image from the "Bad Photoshop" and ran it through a glitch generator, producing the image you see now! I already experiemented with distortion while creating the original image. I did this by copying and pasting components from the lego bouquet to create a larger bouquet. After doing this, I copied and pasted the bouquet on top of itself 2 times, and had them to the left and right of the center bouquet. Then, I put lego flowers in the background of the image to create balance. I like that the added distortion brings an entirely different viewing context for the piece.
